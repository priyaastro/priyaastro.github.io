I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Center for Astrophysics | Harvard & Smithsonian. I
develop tools and spectral diagnostic methods describing various atomic processes for collisionally-ionized and photoionized astrophysical plasma to interpret  high-resolution X-ray spectra in a variety of astrophysical systems like galaxy clusters, X-ray binaries, AGNs, and galaxies.  Currently, I am a developer of AtomDB,  an atomic database designed for X-ray plasma spectral modeling. I have also been a developer of the spectral synthesis code CLOUDY.
